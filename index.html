Process

How Vectra AI Works.

From signal detection to execution — fully automated, fully transparent.

// 01
📡
Signal Detection

Every few minutes, the engine scans your configured pairs — reading RSI, MACD, BB, VWAP, sentiment scores, order book imbalance, and funding rates simultaneously.

// 02
🧠
AI Scoring

Signals are weighted and scored. Only when enough indicators align in the same direction — above your minimum signal threshold — does the system consider an entry.

// 03
⚡
Execution

Orders are placed instantly at configured position size and leverage. Stop-loss and take-profit levels are set automatically. No hesitation, no second-guessing, no emotion.

// 04
📊
Monitoring & Control

Your dashboard updates in real time. Adjust risk controls, review the trade log, export CSVs, or override settings at any time — without stopping the bot.
